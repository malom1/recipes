Hard Candy

Description

This is a step-by-step procedure on how to make Hard Candy

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • White Sugar
  • Water
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Corn Syrup
  • Orange Color

Steps

  1. Grease a cookie sheet with cooking spray.
  2. Stir together white sugar, corn syrup, and water in a medium saucepan. Cook, stirring, over medium heat until sugar dissolves, then bring to a boil. Without stirring, heat to 300 to 310 degrees F (149 to 154 degrees C), or until a small amount of syrup dropped into cold water forms hard, brittle threads.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir in flavored extract and food coloring. Pour onto the prepared cookie sheet. Dust top with confectioners' sugar.
  4. Let cool until hardened, about 15 minutes. Break into about 36 pieces and store in an airtight container.
